Intro to Anemia

Anemia is a condition where your body lacks enough healthy red blood cells to carry oxygen to tissues. Iron deficiency anemia is the most common type worldwide, affecting energy levels and overall health.

⚠️

Common Symptoms

  • 😴 Persistent Fatigue
  • 🤍 Pale or Yellowish Skin
  • 🌀 Dizziness & Headaches
  • 💅 Brittle Nails
🧠

Impact on Health

Energy: Reduced oxygen transport decreases energy levels.
Focus: Cognitive function and concentration may suffer.
Early Detection: AI-powered screening helps identify anemia early for timely intervention.
